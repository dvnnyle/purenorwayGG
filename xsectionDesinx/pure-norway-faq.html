Sustainability
The PURENorway Foundation is our commitment to the planet in action. We donate 1% of all turnover directly to projects and organisations focused on removing plastic from the world's oceans and preserving marine ecosystems for future generations.
Yes. Our entire production and distribution process runs on renewable Norwegian hydroelectric energy. Combined with aluminum's closed-loop recyclability, our packaging achieves a zero net carbon footprint.
Simply rinse and place in your local aluminum or metal recycling bin. In Norway, you can return cans through the pant (deposit return) system. Aluminum is one of the most recycled materials in the world — recycling a single can saves enough energy to power a TV for three hours.
