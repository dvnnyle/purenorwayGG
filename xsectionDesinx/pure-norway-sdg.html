Pure Norway is committed to the UN Sustainable Development Goals. Here's how our work connects to a global mission — and what we're actively doing about it.

Responsible Consumption & Production
Responsible Consumption & Production

Aluminium is infinitely recyclable — our cans can be melted down and reborn without any loss of quality. By choosing aluminium over plastic, we're eliminating single-use packaging from the equation entirely.

Every can we produce carries a zero carbon footprint. We hold ourselves to this standard not as a marketing claim, but as a production requirement.

14
Life Below Water
Life Below Water

Pure Norway care about nature, especially pure and clean waters and for the life in it. The world's oceans are the source of life — pollution and plastic waste are critical threats that need to be addressed urgently.

We fund this goal directly through the Pure Norway Foundation. A percentage of the income from every product sold goes toward cleaning the coastline of Norway and protecting marine ecosystems.

16
Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions
Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions

Everyone connected to our products — from production workers to administrative staff — is treated with respect and dignity. We have clear standards against child labour, involuntary labour, coercion, harassment and discrimination.

We also prioritise environmentally friendly and recycled materials across our supply chain, reducing unnecessary waste and limiting our impact on the natural world.
